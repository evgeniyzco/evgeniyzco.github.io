INFORMATION WE PROCESS

There are two general categories of information we can process.

II.1 Information that you submit

We process the following personal information about you when you use the App. This information is necessary for the adequate performance of the contract between you and us. Without this information it is impossible to provide complete functionality of the App and perform the requested services: 1) Authorization and/or Account Information. When you create/sign up for an account, we require your email. 2) Other Information directly related to your use of the App, such as photos and documents you upload to the App. Upon your consent we also may have access to your camera roll.

II.2 Automatically Processed Information

When you use the App, some information about your device and your user behaviour may be processed automatically. This information is generally non-personal, meaning it does not allow direct association with any specific individual. We process this information on the ground of our legitimate interest in improving our App and giving our users the best experience. If we do not access such data we may not be able to provide you with all the requested services and features of the App.

We use third-party automatic data processing technologies which helps us analyze information from your device or the App. Automatic data processing technologies (advertising or analytics tools) provide us with the data described below. We do not control, supervise or respond for how the third parties process your personal data, that might be collected by their own means (not through our App). As some of our third parties declare themselves as controllers for GDPR purposes, any requests regarding your personal data should be directed to these third parties.

Device-related information: When you use a mobile device (tablet / phone / smartwatch) to access our App, we may monitor one or more "device identifiers". Device identifiers are small data files or similar data structures stored on or associated with your mobile device, which uniquely identify your mobile device. Device identifier enables generalized reporting or personalized content and ads by the third parties. Data that may be processed include:
  1. Your mobile device type and the operating system versionwhich is installed on your mobile device;
  2. Your mobile device's regional settings;
  3. The region tied to your SIM card;
  4. Your Internet Protocol ("IP") address;
  5. An Identity For Advertisers/Advertising ID, which is a string of numbers and letters that identifies your individual smartphone or tablet.
Log File Data: Automatically captured each time you request access to the App and when the App is installed on your device. Our analytics tools also automatically record specific log file data, such as the time and date of your App usage, along with your interaction with the App.

The App may engage in automated processing of your personal data, including profiling, which is used to analyze or predict personal preferences, interests, behavior, location, or movements. This process begins automatically when you start using the App.

Cookies and Similar Technologies: To improve your experience, we may use cookies and technologies like pixels and web beacons when you use the App. A cookie is a small file downloaded to your device upon accessing the App, which is then sent back to our servers with each use of the App. This helps us operate the App efficiently, understanding which areas or features are popular and the content users are clicking on. We may partner with advertisers or other third parties to deliver ads and services in the App, which may use cookies or similar technologies.

4) To communicate with you. We use the information we have to communicate with you through newsletters, marketing notifications, to receive your feedback about our App, and respond to you when you contact us.

Opt-Out of the Location Data Processing

Some third-party advertising partners may collect precise location data or street-level location information about you ("Precise Location Data"). Your mobile device may allow you to opt out of the collection of Precise Location Data as described below.

For iOS 11 or later: To disable the collection of Precise Location Data on iOS, you can turn off Location Services for the applicable Product via "Settings > Privacy > Location Services".
